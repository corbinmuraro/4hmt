4-H Million Trees Project

  • Give 6 million 4-H youth around the world the opportunity to get personally involved in the climate crisis
  • Reduce atmospheric CO2 while having fun planting millions of trees around the world
  • Provide youth with leadership opportunities
  • Encourage youth to start service-learning projects in areas they are passionate about

Progress

The 4-H Million Trees Project currently consists of:

  • 162,238 members
  • From 629 Clubs/Units
  • Spread across 321 counties in 59 US states and other nations

Together we have:

  • Planted 1,274,538 trees
  • Plan to plant an additional 3,590,012 trees
